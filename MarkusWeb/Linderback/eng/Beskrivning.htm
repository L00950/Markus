Welcome to Nedre Ryda in the fantastic landscape of southern Östergötland. The estate is situated between the beautiful lake Åsunden and the forest lake Nedre Ucknen. There is a wide variety of leisure activities including sunbathing, fishing and going for walks in the forest where you can pick berries and wild mushrooms.

Our picturesque red timbered cottages with room for four (six) people is tastefully renovated and fully fitted with all modern conveniences. The cottages have own gardens with garden furnitures and barbecues. The distance between the two cottages is about 100 meters. It is possible to rent bicycles and boats with outboard motors in lake Åsunden.
