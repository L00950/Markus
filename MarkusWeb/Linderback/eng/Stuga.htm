From the cottage you have a marvellous view which overlooks meadows, fields and a mountain ridge. The cottage has its own garden with garden furniture and a barbecue. In the outhouse there are room for storing fishing equipment and other things.

The cottage is electrically heated and has hot and cold water (all included in the rent). The cottage has two floors which are 40 square meters altogether. Downstairs there is a living room with a corner sofa (convertable into a twin-bed), sofa table, TV with satellite and dining table.

The charming kitchen with its pinewood interior is fully equipped with electric cooker, oven, microwave oven, coffee maker, refrigerator (with freezing-compartment) and kitchen/cooking utensils.

The steep wooden stairs lead you to the upper floor.

Upstairs there is a sloping ceiling high enough to stand upright in the middle, giving it a charming ambience. There is a double-bed and two single-beds, a bathroom with toilet and shower. Mattresses, quilts and pillows are supplied. Linen and towels are not provided.
