The cottage was completelly renovated in 2006 and has a high and beatiful position in front of the wood. It has it´s own garden with garden furniture and a barbecue.

The cottage has two floors which are 56 square meters altogether. Downstairs there is a living room with a kitchen-section and a bath-room with toilet and shower. In the living room there is a corner sofa (convertible into a twin-bed), sofa table, TV, a dining table and a kitchen-corner. The kitchen is equipped with electric cooker, oven, coffee maker, refrigerator and kitchen/cooking utensils.

Upstairs there is a sloping ceiling with visible ceiling balks. There are two bedrooms. Mattresses, quilts and pillows are supplied. Linen and towels are not provided.
